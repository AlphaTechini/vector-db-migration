Frequently Asked Questions

Which vector databases do you support?

We support bidirectional migration between Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, and Milvus. Additional databases (Chroma, FAISS, pgvector) are on the roadmap for late 2026.

How long does migration take?

For 1M vectors: 2-4 hours. For 10M vectors: 1-2 days. For 100M+ vectors: 3-7 days. Timeline depends on schema complexity and validation requirements.

Is there downtime during migration?

No. Our dual-write sync keeps both databases in sync during migration. You switch traffic with a single command, with instant rollback if needed.

How do you validate embeddings?

We sample 10K vectors pre/post migration and verify cosine similarity >0.98. Full validation reports include recall metrics, latency comparisons, and index statistics.

What about security compliance?

Enterprise plan includes SOC 2 Type II audit reports, HIPAA BAA, GDPR data processing agreements, and encryption in transit/at rest. All migrations are logged for audit trails.

Can I try before buying?

Yes! We offer a free trial migration for up to 10K vectors. No credit card required. This lets you validate the process before committing.
